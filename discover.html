Who Are We?

ACE (Association of Computer Engineers) is the official student-led technical body under the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, SRKR Engineering College. It acts as the bridge between students and innovation.

Our Vision

To foster creativity, leadership, and real-world skills in CSE students by providing a collaborative platform for innovation and growth.

Our Mission

To empower students with technical knowledge, organize events that stimulate learning, and build a thriving developer culture in SRKR.

Major Milestones

  • 2021: ACE launched with 3 events and 60+ participants.
  • 2022: Hosted SRKR’s first in-house Figma design workshop.
  • 2023: Introduced Beats & Code and SkillUp mentoring sessions.
  • 2024: Prajwalam Hackathon & Spurthi 2K24 — 1000+ turnout!
  • 2025: ACE expanded into AI, ML, and innovation-focused tracks.
